ROCHAK BASNET

STUDENT

Hi, I'm Rochak Basnet, a freshman student at the University of Southern Mississippi majoring in Media and Entertainment Arts (Video Arts) and a minor in Theatre. I was born in Kathmandu, Nepal. I aspire to become a filmmaker. Currently, I make videos on Youtube and have designed websites for Khelxa, and Lekhxa. In my free time, I enjoy designing, making videos, cooking, dancing, and travelling. I love watching movies and series. I'm passionate about exploring new opportunities to improve my craft and push boundaries in all aspects of my life.
